Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2026

Pix2Paper is a document scanner and image-to-PDF converter app. This Privacy Policy explains how Pix2Paper handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Pix2Paper does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app may request access to your device camera and photos/media only so you can scan documents, select images, and convert them into PDF files.

2. Camera and Photo Access

Pix2Paper may use your device camera to capture document images.

Pix2Paper may access selected images from your device gallery when you choose to convert images into PDF files.

Images, scans, and PDF files are processed locally on your device. Pix2Paper does not upload your images, scanned documents, or PDF files to any server.

3. Files and Documents

PDF files created with Pix2Paper are stored locally on your device.

You are in control of your files. You may open, rename, share, or delete your generated PDF files from your device.

Pix2Paper does not read, transfer, sell, or share your documents with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

Pix2Paper does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Pix2Paper does not share your personal information, images, scans, or PDF files with third parties.

When you choose to share a PDF file, the sharing action is controlled by your device and the app you choose to share with.

5. Internet Usage

Pix2Paper is designed to work offline. The app does not require an internet connection for scanning documents or converting images to PDF.

6. Children’s Privacy

Pix2Paper is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

Pix2Paper processes files locally on your device. Please protect your device with a password, PIN, fingerprint, or other security method to keep your documents safe.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
